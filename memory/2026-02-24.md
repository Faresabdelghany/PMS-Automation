### Key Lessons
- **Vercel can't reach localhost** — any gateway proxy that hits localhost:18789 from server-side will fail. Use DB-based status checks instead.
- **Model restrictions on cron jobs** — only certain models are "allowed" for OpenClaw cron jobs. gemini-2.0-flash and claude-haiku-3-5 are blocked. groq/llama works.
- **system-event cron type** — fires in main session as a message, not as a background script. Not suitable for auto-sync.
- **Windows Startup folder** — `shell:startup` is the easy way to auto-start daemons without admin rights.
